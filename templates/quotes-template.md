# Quotes Template

Use this template for extracting quotable moments for clips or social posts.

---

## Identification Criteria

Look for moments that are:

- Insightful — reveals a deeper truth
- Strong opinions — bold or contrarian takes
- Memorable analogies — makes a complex idea simple
- Practical wisdom — immediately applicable advice

## Output Format

```markdown
# Quotes: [Title]

## Top Quotes

### [MM:SS] "[Exact quote]"

- **Type:** insight | hot-take | wisdom | story | humor
- **Shareability:** high | medium | low
- **Context:** [1 sentence explaining when/why this was said]

### [MM:SS] "[Exact quote]"

- **Type:** insight | hot-take | wisdom | story | humor
- **Shareability:** high | medium | low
- **Context:** [1 sentence]
```

## Notes

- For text without timestamps, omit the timestamp and use sequential numbering (`#1`, `#2`, etc.).
- Use exact words when possible — paraphrase only when the original is too long or unclear.
- Aim for 5-15 quotes depending on content length.
- Sort by shareability (high first) within each section.
